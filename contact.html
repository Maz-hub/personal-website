Check-out the Swiss Riviera map

Nestled along the shores of Lake Geneva, the Swiss Riviera is world-renowned for its Montreux Jazz Festival, attracting music enthusiasts from around the world. It’s also home to the famous Lavaux vineyards, a UNESCO World Heritage site. The region’s charm extends beyond nature—with stunning views of the Alps meeting the lake—and history comes alive with landmarks like Château de Chillon. Plus, it’s where Charlie Chaplin spent his final years, and his former home, now the Chaplin’s World museum, offers a fascinating glimpse into his life.
